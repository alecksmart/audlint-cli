# Code of Conduct

## Expected behavior
- Be respectful and concise.
- Focus on technical facts.
- Assume good intent.

## Unacceptable behavior
- Harassment, threats, or personal attacks.
- Discriminatory language.
- Spam, trolling, or deliberate disruption.

## Enforcement
Project maintainers may remove comments, close threads, or block participants
who violate this policy.
